About Me

Hi! I'm Rati, an experimental filmmaker, animator, and illustration artist. I dive into all things quirky and creative, bringing ideas to life in ways that surprise and inspire!

My work revolves around the weird, the imperfect, and the beautifully bizarre. I find joy in storytelling, whether through frame-by-frame animation, sketchbooks, or vivid paintings.

Animals, emotions, and the unseen magic of life fuel my creativity. My dream? To tell stories that make you feel something real.
